📜 How to Play Wordle

Guess the hidden **5-letter word** within **6 attempts**!

🎯 Game Rules:

  • Each guess must be a valid **5-letter** word.
  • Press **Enter** to submit your guess.
  • After submitting, the tiles will change color to give you hints:
W
**Green** - Correct letter & correct position.
A
**Yellow** - Correct letter but wrong position.
S
**Gray** - Letter is not in the word at all.

📝 Additional Rules:

  • You cannot enter **random** or **made-up** words.
  • If you solve the word, you can move to the next one.
  • If you fail after 6 attempts, the correct word is revealed.
  • Click on the **Previous** button to revisit past words.

🔹 Example Play:

Suppose the word is **PLUMB** and you guess **PLANE**, the colors will be:

P
L
A
N
E

Now, you know **P & L** are correct, **A** exists in another spot, and **N & E** are wrong.
